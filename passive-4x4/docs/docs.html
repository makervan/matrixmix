MatrixMix 4x4

by MakerVan Labs

Hey there!

You just got yourself the PCB to a MakerVan Labs MatrixMix 4x4. Let's get you started with it.

Parts

You'll need a few additional parts for your MatrixMix 4x4:

  • Eight 3.5mm stereo jacks
  • Sixteen potentiometers (Suggestion: Log 50kΩ)
  • Sixteen resistors (Suggestion: 10kΩ)

You can populate P1-P8 with 3-pin 2.54mm headers, and mix the two channels of a stereo plug together, or pipe the second channel through to other devices, in a mode compatible with the Pocket Operator synths or similar modules.

Connectors P9 and P10 can be used to connect multiple MatrixMix 4x4 boards together to create a matrix mixer with 8, 12 or even more inputs.

Attention!

This is a passive mixer. So when you change one knob, there might be changes of loudness for the other channels as well. There’s no buffering between the channels, so it’s completely normal like that.
